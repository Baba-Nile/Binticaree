BintiCare is dedicated to advancing the education, wellbeing, and leadership of girls and young women. Through mentorship, access to resources, and long-term support, we help create pathways to opportunity, confidence, and lasting impact in communities.

WHO WE ARE

Advancing opportunity, dignity, and equality

BintiCare Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to empowering girls and young women through education, mentorship, and access to opportunity. Since 2012, we have worked to create pathways that enable girls to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity and accountability in all our programs and partnerships
  • Transparency and openness to continuous learning and improvement
  • Equity-focused support for underserved and vulnerable communities
  • Community participation in designing sustainable, local solutions
OUR FOUNDATION

The BintiCare Foundation Pillars

Our work is guided by three core pillars that shape our approach to empowerment, ensuring every girl experiences dignity, direction, and belonging.

Dignity

Every girl deserves respect, safety, and the opportunity to thrive as a valued individual.

Purpose

We help girls discover direction through mentorship, education, and life skills development.

Belonging

We create safe, supportive environments where girls feel connected, valued, and empowered.

OUR INITIATIVES

Driving Impact Through Action

Our programs are designed to restore dignity, strengthen resilience, and create sustainable pathways for girls and young women to thrive.

Outreach Programs

We engage communities through awareness and support programs that promote dignity, health, and empowerment.

  • Dignity awareness sessions
  • Community medical camps
  • Maternity support initiatives
  • Prison outreach programs

Rescue & Protection

We provide immediate care, protection, and rehabilitation for vulnerable girls, ensuring safety and access to long-term support.

  • Safe housing (Binti House)
  • Education reintegration programs
  • Community rescue outreach

Skills & Capacity Building

We equip girls and young women with practical skills to support economic independence and long-term self-reliance.

  • Satellite training hubs
  • Youth & teen mother programs
