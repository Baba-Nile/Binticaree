UNIFORM A CHILD

Dressing a child with dignity, confidence, and belonging.

Uniform a Child is a program under the BintiCare Foundation dedicated to supporting children by providing school uniforms that promote dignity, confidence, and a sense of belonging. Through this initiative, we aim to remove barriers to education and ensure that every child can learn with pride and equal opportunity.

What We Do

  • School uniforms to ensure dignity and equality among learners
  • School shoes for comfort, protection, and daily use
  • School bags to carry books and learning materials safely
